The allure of a high-performance luxury coupe is undeniable. It whispers promises of exhilarating acceleration, head-turning style, and a driving experience that elevates the mundane to the extraordinary. And few cars embody this spirit more perfectly than the Audi S5.

A Symphony of Power and Precision

Under the hood of the 2025 S5 beats a heart of pure performance: a 3.0-liter, turbocharged V6 engine capable of unleashing a staggering 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. This potent powerplant, paired with Audi’s renowned quattro all-wheel drive system, transforms the S5 into a true performance machine.

The S5 doesn’t just accelerate; it catapults. The 0-60 mph sprint is dispatched in a mere 4.5 seconds, a testament to the car’s raw power and precise handling. The eight-speed automatic transmission shifts seamlessly, providing both lightning-fast acceleration and smooth, effortless cruising.

A Masterclass in Handling and Control

Beyond raw power, the S5 excels in handling. Audi’s renowned quattro all-wheel drive system provides exceptional grip and stability, allowing you to tackle corners with confidence and precision. The S5’s suspension, meticulously tuned for both comfort and performance, ensures a smooth ride without compromising handling.

The steering is responsive and precise, offering a direct connection to the road. The brakes are powerful and fade-resistant, providing the stopping power needed to match the S5’s exhilarating performance.

A Blend of Sport and Sophistication

The 2025 S5 doesn’t just offer thrilling performance; it does so in an environment of unparalleled luxury and refinement. Step inside the S5’s cabin, and you’ll be greeted by a symphony of high-quality materials, meticulous craftsmanship, and cutting-edge technology.

The sport seats, wrapped in premium leather, offer both comfort and support. The dashboard, featuring a driver-focused design, is a masterpiece of ergonomics and functionality. The S5’s interior is a testament to Audi’s commitment to creating a driving experience that is as luxurious as it is exhilarating.

Technology at Your Fingertips

The 2025 S5 is equipped with a suite of advanced technologies designed to enhance both your driving experience and your safety. The Audi virtual cockpit, a fully digital instrument cluster, provides a customizable and highly informative display. The MMI infotainment system, with its intuitive interface and advanced features, keeps you connected and entertained.

The S5 also boasts a comprehensive suite of driver-assistance features, including adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, blind spot monitoring, and more. These features work together to provide a safer and more enjoyable driving experience.
